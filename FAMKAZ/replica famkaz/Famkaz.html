Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got detailed answers! Here's everything you need to know about your spiritual journey with Famkaz Travel.

What is included in the Hajj package?

Our comprehensive Hajj package includes visa processing, round-trip flights, premium accommodations, delicious meals, seamless transportation, guided tours, and expert-led seminars to ensure a fulfilling experience.

How does the “Pay Small Small” plan work?

Our flexible payment plans allow you to spread the cost of your pilgrimage over 6, 10, or 12 months, or make random payments at your convenience until the travel period. Choose what fits your budget!

Can I customize my travel dates for Umrah?

Absolutely! We offer flexible travel dates for Umrah, subject to availability. Contact our team to discuss your preferred schedule.

What documents are required for visa processing?

You’ll need a valid passport, recent passport-sized photos, and other standard documents. Our team will guide you through the process and handle all paperwork for a hassle-free experience.

Are the seminars mandatory?

No, but we highly recommend attending our weekly Hajj seminars, available both online and in-person, to prepare spiritually, mentally, and practically for your journey.

What safety measures are in place during the pilgrimage?

We prioritize your safety with experienced guides, secure transportation, and accommodations that meet international standards. Our team is available 24/7 to assist you.
