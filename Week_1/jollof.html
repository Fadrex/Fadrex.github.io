How to Prepare The Perfect Jollof Rice in 1 Hour

The Jollof war between Nigeria and Ghana over the past few decades has been at a stalemate, with neither of their indegenes agreeing to surrender the mantle to their opposition. No one wants to back off

However, it's important to note that this delicious delicacy does not just prepare itself. while Ghanian Jollof has a trademark appearance of always appearing reddish and smoky, Nigerian Jollof is always less extravagant, but has a dinstinct quality of always tasting exquisite

Here is a Brilliant guide on how to prepare the tastiest Jollof Rice

Ingredients needed to prepare Jollof Rice

  • rice
  • Salt
  • Tomato paste and spices
  • Groundnut oil
  • Onions
  • Seasoning

Steps on How to cook a pot of Jollof Rice

STEP 1

Make sure you wash your rice and other utensils needed for the cooking process. After washing your rice, put and empty pot on Fire and allow to heat for about two minutes.

STEP 2

Pour in your groundnut oil of about Half a cup and watch it get hot for about a minute. proceed to dice your onions and fresh pepper

STEP 3

After dicing your ingredients, pour into the pot to fry for a while, add a teaspoon of salt and seasoning, pour 4 cups of water and allow to boil

STEP 4

After boiling for 30 minutes, Add your rice, and leave to boil for about 15 minutes, when the rice is almost done, pour in your perfecly sliced onions into it. leave to steam

STEP 5

Your sweet Jollof rice is ready...

While this process might be a bit tasking, the taste justifies the effort put in, and a perfect meal is what you get after an hour. Enjoy...
